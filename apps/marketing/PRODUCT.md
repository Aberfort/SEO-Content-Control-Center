# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

SEO agencies, content teams, and WordPress publishers. Users need to connect WordPress, Google Search Console, and their SEO workflow to locate traffic leaks, prioritize fixes, and prove impact without managing data in spreadsheets.

## Product Purpose

SEO Content Control Center connects WordPress, Google Search Console, and your SEO workflow so teams can find traffic leaks, prioritize fixes, and prove the impact of their work. Success is measured by weekly resolved high-impact SEO tasks per active organization.

## Positioning

SEO Content Control Center connects WordPress, Google Search Console, and your SEO workflow so teams can find traffic leaks, prioritize fixes, and prove the impact of their work.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary and secondary CTA: Primary CTA is "Request Demo / Start Trial" (integrated with the validated lead webhook flow).
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: SEO Content Control Center: Find traffic leaks, prioritize fixes, and prove the impact of your work.
- Belief ladder:
  1. Managing SEO in spreadsheets and scattered tools (Ahrefs, GSC, WordPress, sheets) is chaotic and causes traffic leaks.
  2. Connecting WordPress and GSC directly into one dashboard creates a single, reliable SEO backlog.
  3. Safe, review-first operations (preview, dry run, human approval, signed execution) make bulk updates safe.
  4. Resolving high-impact SEO tasks weekly directly drives organic growth and is easily verifiable.
- Proof on hand: Validated product facts (number of audit checks, bounded-operations, review-first safety workflow, activity history). No fake social proof, no fictitious testimonials or client logos.

## Brand Personality

Minimalist, quiet, and typographic. Focused on editorial restraint, precision, and clarity. Avoids shouting display styles, loud colors, or generic decorations.

## Anti-references

Generic AI/SaaS slop (purple/blue gradients, blurry neon blobs, glassmorphism, sketchy SVG doodles, diagonal background stripes, tiny wide-tracked uppercase kickers above every section).

## Design Principles

- **Editorial Restraint**: Quiet confidence through clean typography and ample negative space.
- **Safety First**: Clear confirmation layers (preview -> dry run -> confirmation -> execution) shown visually in illustrations and interface details.
- **Precision over Magic**: Show real, measurable workflow operations instead of promising magical ranking increases.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Requires a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for body copy, visible focus indicator states for all interactive elements, and strict prefers-reduced-motion styling for all animations and transitions.
